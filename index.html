About

Local Soundcheck is a hobby project designed with two simple goals:

#1 Help me quickly find bands I would like to hear live.
#2 Help those bands get more exposure.

How does this site do those two things? It simply scans local venue websites and presents their upcoming acts but with instantaneous music samples grabbed from YouTube. Like it? Then the Get Tickets button takes you directly to the venue's website so you can see their buying options. Fast and simple.

I hope you enjoy. Have fun. — Bill
