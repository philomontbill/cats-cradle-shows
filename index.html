Local Soundcheck™

Currently featuring: Cat's Cradle, Carrboro NC and Mohawk, Austin TX

I've missed great artists at Cat's Cradle in Chapel Hill, NC because many are up and coming and I don't recognize their name or music. Previewing their music took too much time. This site solves that with one click to hear any artist on YouTube.

  • Main acts are in large white text. Hover and a red play button appears.
  • Opening acts are in smaller blue text. Click to hear them too.
  • Get Tickets takes you directly back to the venue's website for prices and purchase. (BTW I'm not in the middle of any of this. It's a totally free service for you.)

I'm kicking this off using YouTube for music search for two reasons: 1) That's where very new acts are most likely to have content. 2) What's the use of putting a ton of time into adding links to Bandcamp, Spotify and others until I see there's interest? Just sayin.

I hope you enjoy. Send any ideas for improvement. — Bill
